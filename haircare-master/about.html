About Us

Welcome To Classic Hair & Beauty Training Academy.

Our company’s name was derived from the English phrase “Classic”, meaning that we provide highest quality and outstanding of its kind.

The name was specifically chosen to reflect our pride in our South African roots and signifies our commitment to creating a company that is truly committed in quality in whatever we do.

Mission statement

At Classic Hair & Beauty Training Academy, we pledge to provide distinctive quality and supreme customer service as we strive to gain the respect and trust of our clients, suppliers.

Vision statement

Classic Hair & Beauty Training Academy, Skills Development is the preferred provider of education training and development for Hair and Beauty in South Africa"

Hair & Beauty

Training Academy

Our proficient teams, consisting of well-trained facilitators, assessors, and moderators, engage with delegates at a level that ensures comfort, delving into everyday scenarios to contextualize our invaluable training. This approach enhances the preservation of the knowledge we impart. Our training methodology is anchored in principles of good governance, aligning with accreditation requirements and demonstrating a robust adherence to the Health & Safety Act.

Employing a modular competency system in line with the National Qualifications Framework (NQF), our training materials are meticulously compiled, ensuring the implementation of effective training. You can trust in the assurance that Classic Hair Academy Skills is a comprehensive service provider committed to carrying out all mandatory training in compliance with current legislation.

Notably, Classic Hair Academy proudly stands as a 100% female-owned institution, further exemplifying our commitment to diversity and excellence in our educational offerings.

Internal Accreditation

All Facilitators Classic Hair & Beauty Training Academy are accredited as Assessors & Moderators. Facilitators reports will be monitored, and spot checks will be done by our managers e.g.: by sitting in on training interventions or assessing students on completion of the attended course. All facilitators presenting customised courses are involved in the course design and development to ensure they have the necessary background knowledge.
