Our values & beliefs

We are committed to upholding absolute integrity in all our endeavors. Collaborating closely with our clients, we foster a partnership approach. Our organizational culture thrives on active participation, leveraging the inherent excellence of our people and providing an environment where they can truly flourish. Supporting our team's growth, we focus on enabling them to reach their full potential through ongoing education, training, and acknowledging outstanding performance. Informed by data, our decision-making process is geared towards maximizing opportunities for success. Acknowledging our role as responsible citizens and neighbors, we actively engage with and contribute to the communities in which we operate. Ensuring the seamless fulfillment of our requirements, we cultivate advantageous partnerships with our suppliers. Driven by an unwavering commitment to continuous improvement, we passionately strive to enhance our processes, products, and services.

Clients

In our business philosophy, the client takes center stage as the utmost priority. Recognizing that we are not independent of them but rather dependent on their patronage, we understand that their presence is not an interruption but the very purpose of our work. A client, to us, is an individual who entrusts us with their needs, and it becomes our responsibility to fulfill those requirements. Regardless of the nature of their demands, each client is entitled to the highest level of courtesy and attention. It is the client who, through their support, enables us to meet our financial obligations, ensuring that salaries are paid and needs are addressed, emphasizing their pivotal role as the driving force behind our business.

Business purpose

The core purpose of Classic Hair & Beauty Training Academy is unequivocally defined as "to create and assist clients." At our academy, the clients hold the key to shaping our business identity. They constitute the bedrock, the very foundation of our enterprise, being the ones who not only dictate our business essence but also sustain our workforce by providing employment and facilitating salary payments. In our customer-centric approach, rudeness is never tolerated, misinformation is strictly avoided, and the phrase "I don't know" is deemed unacceptable. When faced with uncertainty, it becomes a professional obligation to promptly seek the necessary information from a reliable source and relay it back to the customer at the earliest convenience. Embodying these principles, the following values statements serve as the guiding principles at the core of our business, adhered to with unwavering commitment to ensure the success of our enterprise.

Our proficient teams, consisting of well-trained facilitators, assessors, and moderators, engage with delegates at a level that ensures comfort, delving into everyday scenarios to contextualize our invaluable training. This approach enhances the preservation of the knowledge we impart. Our training methodology is anchored in principles of good governance, aligning with accreditation requirements and demonstrating a robust adherence to the Health & Safety Act.
